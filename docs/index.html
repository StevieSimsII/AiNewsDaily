About AI News Daily

An automated AI and machine learning news aggregator built for a focused daily read.

What it tracks

AI News Daily collects articles, research, and industry analysis from leading technology and research sources, then filters for artificial intelligence and machine learning relevance.

How it works

A scheduled collector fetches source feeds, normalizes article metadata, filters for topical relevance, and publishes a static CSV-backed site.

Sources

Sources include major technology publications, AI labs, research organizations, and analyst firms such as Gartner and Forrester.

Why this layout

The interface mirrors the Second Brain reading pattern: persistent navigation, immediate search, compact filters, and dense cards designed for scanning.
